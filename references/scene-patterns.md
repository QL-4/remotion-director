# Scene Patterns

> Full-scene animation approaches across quality tiers.

## Explainer Video Pattern

**Typical structure:** Problem → Solution → Benefits → CTA

**What you usually see:**
- Title card
- Bullet points or icons with text
- Product screenshot or illustration
- CTA button

---

### Standard Tier: Clean Professional

**Overall mood:** Business presentation, content-first

**Title:** Gentle fade up, 0.5s  
**Bullets:** Staggered fade up, 0.1s apart  
**Product:** Fade + scale, restrained  
**CTA:** Fade up with slight delay  

**Total intro:** 2-3 seconds  
**Feel:** Gets out of the way, lets content shine

**Pitch to user:**
> "Clean and professional—animations support the message 
> without being the message. Good for B2B, tutorials, 
> when credibility matters most."

---

### Enhanced Tier: Polished Engagement

**Overall mood:** Modern product video, premium feel

**Title:** Fade up + scale, smooth  
**Bullets:** Cascade effect (each finishes before next starts)  
**Product:** Slides in from side with shadow  
**CTA:** Bounces in playfully at the end  

**Total intro:** 3-4 seconds  
**Feel:** Engaging, polished, memorable

**Pitch to user:**
> "Premium feel—animations add personality while staying professional. 
> Each element has presence. Good for product launches, 
> marketing videos, when you want engagement."

---

### Cinematic Tier: The Launch

**Overall mood:** Event, announcement, "this is big"

**Title:** Kinetic typography or dramatic word-by-word  
**Bullets:** Wave effect through list  
**Product:** Spins and scales from center  
**CTA:** Dramatic emphasis with glow  

**Total intro:** 4-5 seconds  
**Feel:** Impossible to ignore, shareable

**Pitch to user:**
> "Event-level production—every moment has impact. 
> Good for launches, viral content, when you need 
> viewers to stop scrolling and watch."

## Product Demo Pattern

**Typical structure:** Product reveal → Features → Use case → CTA

---

### Standard: The Walkthrough

**Product:** Gentle fade + scale  
**Features:** Staggered highlights with connecting lines  
**Use case:** Screenshot transitions, click points  
**CTA:** Fade up, ready for action  

**Pitch:**
> "Clear product walkthrough—animations guide the eye 
> without distraction. Sales-friendly."

---

### Enhanced: The Showcase

**Product:** Slide in with presence  
**Features:** Pop in sequence, coordinated  
**Use case:** Smooth UI animations between states  
**CTA:** Pulse gently while visible  

**Pitch:**
> "Showroom quality—makes the product feel premium 
> and responsive. Conference presentation ready."

---

### Cinematic: The Reveal

**Product:** Dramatic rotation + scale entrance  
**Features:** Sweep reveal coordinated to narration  
**Use case:** Continuous camera-style motion  
**CTA:** Grand entrance with emphasis  

**Pitch:**
> "Apple-style reveal—every frame feels considered. 
> For when the product IS the moment."

---

## Data Visualization Pattern

**Typical structure:** Context → Chart/statistics → Insight

---

### Standard: The Report

**Chart:** Bars grow up, staggered 0.1s  
**Numbers:** Counter animation  
**Insight:** Fade in as highlight  

**Pitch:**
> "Clear data storytelling—viewers understand the chart 
> as it builds. Professional reports."

---

### Enhanced: The Dashboard

**Chart:** Liquid fill effect  
**Numbers:** Count up with easing  
**Insight:** Highlight animates with glow  

**Pitch:**
> "Modern dashboard feel—data feels alive and current. 
> Good for presentations that need energy."

---

### Cinematic: The Moment

**Chart:** Sweep line reveals progressively  
**Numbers:** Dramatic counter with scaling  
**Insight:** Spotlight effect on key finding  

**Pitch:**
> "The metric drop—when that number needs to feel 
> like an achievement. Milestone videos."

---

## Social Media Pattern

**Typical structure:** Hook (0-3s) → Value → CTA (final 2s)

---

### Standard: The Efficient

**Hook:** Quick fade up (0.3s)  
**Value:** Cuts between points  
**CTA:** Immediate appearance  

**Pitch:**
> "Gets to the point—respects the scroll. 
> Good for informational content."

---

### Enhanced: The Engaging

**Hook:** Scale pop with energy  
**Value:** Slide transitions, snappy  
**CTA:** Bouncy entrance  

**Pitch:**
> "Stops the scroll—animation energy matches 
> the platform. TikTok/Instagram ready."

---

### Cinematic: The Viral

**Hook:** Dramatic kinetic type or zoom  
**Value:** Coordinated motion graphics  
**CTA:** Pulse + glow demanding attention  

**Pitch:**
> "Algorithm bait—every frame demands attention. 
> For when views matter most."

---

## Testimonial Pattern

**Typical structure:** Quote → Person → Results

---

### Standard: The Professional

**Quote:** Fade up in full  
**Person:** Gentle fade + scale  
**Results:** Simple counter  

**Pitch:**
> "Puts focus on the words—testimonial is credible 
> and clear. Corporate style."

---

### Enhanced: The Story

**Quote:** Word-by-word or line reveal  
**Person:** Slide in with presence  
**Results:** Count up with emphasis  

**Pitch:**
> "Narrative unfolding—draws viewers into the story. 
> Customer spotlight feel."

---

### Cinematic: The Spotlight

**Quote:** Dramatic word timing  
**Person:** Spotlight reveal  
**Results:** Counter with glow  

**Pitch:**
> "The testimonial IS the production—emotional 
> and impactful. Case study centerpiece."
